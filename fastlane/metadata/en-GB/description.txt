Touch is a free app for the iPhone and iPad that keeps you up-to-date with Touch news; read about and listen to tracks from the Touch catalogue; stream each and every Touch Radio episode; save Jon Wozencroft's Touch cover art as wallpaper and cook meals from the Touch Recipe Book. 

Artists released on Touch include Oren Ambarchi, Biosphere, Eleh, Fennesz, Soliman Gamil, Hildur Gudnadottir, Ryoji Ikeda, Philip Jeck, Phill Niblock, BJNilsen, Rosy Parlane, Sohrab, Mika Vainio, CM von Hausswolff, Chris Watson and Jana Winderen.
